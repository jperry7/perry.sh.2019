WHAT IS IEEE?

The Intitute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers is an internation association for technical professionals with over 430,000 members including computer scientists, software developers, information technology professionals, physicists, medical doctors, electrical engineers, electronics engineers, and students.

IEEE NSM

Being a student is the first job of your professional career. Joining this student chapter of IEEE will unlock resources that will accelarate your career plans. IEEE members gain access to thousands of publications that can be used to supplement your education and keep abreast of new and changing technology.

IEEE student members can apply for scholarships and award programs and can compete in competitions such as IEEExtreme. They can receive discounts on technical resources and reduced registration fees for conferences.

IEEE NSM provides its members with hands-on workshops and seminars. We also invite companies to campus to give talks to students in events called Lunch and Learns. These are fantastic opportunities to network with potential employers and learn how to prepare for a professional career.



IEEEXtreme

IEEEXtreme is a 24-hour online coding competition where college students compete in teams of up to three to complete unique programming challenges. The Grand Prize is a trip to the IEEE conference of your choice, anywhere in the world. The competition is held annually in October.
